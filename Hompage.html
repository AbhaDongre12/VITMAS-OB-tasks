VITMAS

VIT Mathematics Association

VITMAS is VIT Vellore's Mathematical chapter. This chapter organises events relating to applications of Maths in fields like AI ML, Data Science, IOT, Cyber Security etc. The board organises project cycles for its members. They participate in all major events in VIT like GraVITas, Riviera and Yantra.
